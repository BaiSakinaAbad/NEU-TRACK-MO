# Track Mo: Feature Guide 🚀

Welcome to **Track Mo**! This guide explains the key features available to you based on your role. We've designed the system to be intuitive, secure, and helpful for everyone at New Era University.

---

### 🎓 For Students
*   **Find Internships:** Browse a live list of all approved and active university partnerships (Host Training Establishments).
*   **Smart Search:** Use the search bar to filter companies by name or industry type to find the best fit for your OJT.
*   **Real-time Status:** Only see "Approved" and "Valid" partnerships, ensuring you always have accurate information for placements.

### 👩‍🏫 For Faculty
*   **Department Dashboard:** View real-time statistics of your department's MOAs, including those currently in processing.
*   **Partnership Management:** Easily register new agreements or update the details of existing ones.
*   **Recycle Bin:** Move outdated records to a "Bin" and recover them instantly if they were moved by mistake.
*   **Status Tracking:** Monitor where an agreement is in the lifecycle—from legal review to final signing.

### ⚖️ For Admins
*   **Global Overview:** Access the master dashboard showing partnership distribution across all university colleges.
*   **User Governance:** Monitor account activity, track last login timestamps, and block or unblock users to maintain security.
*   **Audit Trail:** View a permanent, immutable log of every single change made in the system (who did what and when).
*   **System Maintenance:** Export logs for accreditation or audit purposes and perform global cleanup tasks.
